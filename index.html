Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a comforting dish made with layers of rich, meaty sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil and sauté the onions and garlic until soft. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes and tomato paste, season with salt and pepper, and let the sauce simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. In a large pot, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, then a layer of ricotta and mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until you’ve used all ingredients.
  6. Top the final layer with Parmesan cheese and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown on top.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A traditional Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Its creamy, flavorful, and quick to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 4 ounces pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and cheese.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving some cooking water, and add it to the pancetta.
  5. Quickly mix in the egg mixture, adding a bit of reserved water to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with parsley and extra cheese.

Tacos

Description

Delicious tacos filled with seasoned meat, fresh toppings, and a squeeze of lime. Perfect for any gathering!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or chicken
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 cup tomato, diced
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Lime wedges

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a skillet until fully cooked. Drain fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water according to package instructions. Simmer for a few minutes.
  3. Warm taco shells in the oven or microwave.
  4. Assemble tacos by filling shells with meat and adding desired toppings.
  5. Serve with lime wedges on the side.
